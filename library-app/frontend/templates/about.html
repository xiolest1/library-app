About Library Search

Our Library Search platform helps you find books and resources easily. We aim to provide quick access to information and make learning more convenient. Explore our collection and discover new knowledge today.

With a user-friendly interface, you can search for books by title, author, subject, or ISBN. Our goal is to provide quick access to information and make learning more convenient and enjoyable for everyone.

Explore our collection, learn something new, and enhance your knowledge today. Join our community of learners and discover a world of information at your fingertips.

This application uses the Open Library API to search for books by Title, Author, Subject, or ISBN. It is built with Flask and demonstrates real-time search along with basic user functions.
